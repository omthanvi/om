About Om Server

Om Server is a Private Limited company committed to bridging the educational gap in underserved communities. We believe that access to quality education, especially through technological advancements, is a fundamental right. Our initiatives focus on providing resources, training, and infrastructure to enhance learning experiences in government schools and remote tribal regions.

Our Vision

A future where every child, regardless of their background or location, has access to empowering education and digital literacy.

Our Mission

To implement sustainable technology solutions and innovative teaching methodologies in government and tribal schools.

Our Values

Integrity, Innovation, Inclusivity, and creativity are at the core of everything we do.

Our Programs

We design and implement comprehensive programs tailored to the unique needs of the communities we serve.

Tech-Enabled Classrooms

We equip government schools with computers and interactive smart boards to create dynamic learning environments.

  • Digital literacy workshops for students
  • E-learning content integration
  • Teacher training on digital boards

Tribal Education Enhancement

Our specialized programs for tribal areas focus on culturally relevant education combined with modern technological exposure.

  • Promoting creativity and reusing of items for science
  • Vocational training with tech skills
  • Community-based learning centers

Meet Our Founder

Mr. Arvind Thanvi

Mr. Arvind Thanvi is the visionary behind Om Server, driven by a profound belief in the transformative power of education and technology. With years of experience in social development and a passion for empowering underserved communities, he founded Om Server to ensure every child has the opportunity to thrive in the digital age. Graduate from IIT Kharagpur, Arvind brings with him 28 years of vast experience in IT Services and Consultancy space with global industry leader firms like Accenture, Goldman Sachs, Barclays etc. His last stint with Accenture lasted for 10 years and at the time of leaving Accenture, he was account director and delivery unit lead for leading UK based banks emerging markets & new business portfolio. He is an educationist by passion as well as an eminent speaker and startup mentor with various Incubation Centers.
